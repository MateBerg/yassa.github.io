Join the group

Candidates looking to join the group should have a good background in mathematics and programming, and provide evidence of scientific writing or publication capability for example a final year project dissertation.

If you are self-funded or have secured external funding, please send me your CV and evidence of your work to christopher.g.willcocks@durham.ac.uk.

If you are a high-achieving candidate at Durham University, you may consider applying for an departmental or faculty studentship. Please contact me in November or early December to discuss your application proposal. For all other candidates, please consult the PhD funding guide for alternative options.
